Our Services

Moving and Packing

We offer various services to ensure your move is as uncomplicated and as smooth as possible we offer a full range of Packing, Moving and Storage services that cover all types of moving. To ensure an accurate estimate, our specialists will need to review your move details with you. An in-home meeting is the best way to complete this, however this can be completed over the phone as well if required. We will go over everything, but importantly if there are any large appliances such as fridges and washers, fragile items such as paintings and lamps, items that require particular handling such as pianos, as well as any packing requirements that are needed for these items.

Storage

For your convenience, we have partnerships with storage facilities across the province and even outside the country. We provide an ideal environment for storing belongings, long or short term. Our specialists pack, organise, code and seal everything into a locked vault which you have complete access to.

Our Care Guarantee
