Our Services

Packing and Moving

We offer a full range of packing, moving and storage options All your needs will be accounted for, including such items as:

  • Fridges and washers
  • Fragile items (i.e. paintings, lamps)
  • Other particular items such as, pianos and any other necessary requirements
  • Provide disassembly and reassembly of items as well as plastic wrap and protective moving blankets

Storage

We are pleased to brag that we have partnership relations across the province and even outside the country. We provide an ideal environment for storing belongings for long or short term, or even for a short stop while waiting to relocate. Our specialists pack, organise, code and seal everything into a locked vault which you have complete access to. These storage facilities are fully insured and security monitored.
